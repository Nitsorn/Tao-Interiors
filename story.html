OUR STORY

"We make doors and windows for a room; but it is the empty spaces that make the room livable" -Lao Tzu

Tao Interiors is a boutique design firm offering the highest quality in interior and exterior material selection, space planning, project management, and design consultation services. Founded in 2011 by interior designer Shima Alavi, and artist John Bland, Tao Interiors was created to address the need for fresh new ideas in the area of west coast interior design.

Since our inception, we have done our best to provide our clients with harmonious and unique environments, while adhering to the highest standards in the industry.Influenced by the sacred wisdom of the Tao, we find our inspiration in the balance and harmony of the natural world around us.

At Tao we are proud to offer our clients a wide range of services in the following areas of expertise:

  • New home construction and design
  • Renovation project management
  • Furniture and decor selection
  • Fine art sales and procurement
  • Furniture re-finishing and repair
  • Space planning, rendering and drafting (CAD)
  • Interior and exterior paint colour consultation
  • Material selection and trades management

To request a quote for any of the above services please fill out a request on our contact page.
